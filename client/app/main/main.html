What We Do


We help you explore the world and discover the perfect site for your current production by geolocating crowd-sourced photos from photographers world-wide. Let Scout.IO show you what the world has to offer from your office.



Features


We're with you every step of the way, from finding the ideal location to managing your project portfolios. We give you the power to quickly and easily look through hundreds of photos that meet your specific criteria. Learn more about a specific location as you scroll through each thumbnail then organize a portfolio for others to see and comment. Whether your journey for the perfect location takes you out into nature or into the city, we're here to help you find it faster.

Search

Search for hundreds of photos using keywords or refine your search by location, radius, and date range using our advanced search feature. Quickly browse through the results in the sidebar or explore the map.

Organize

Found a photo you like?
Save photos in a project portfolio for future viewing. Collaborate with others by sharing your portfolio with team members and leaving each other helpful comments.

Learn

Learn about a location as you browse through search results and view the map. Hover over a thumbnail to see where the photo was taken. Click on the photo to see travel duration, distance and elevation information.
